## Figure 1

Figure 1 Open in figure viewer PowerPoint The neighbour-joining phylogenetic tree depicting the relationship between viral RNA sequences from 83 treatment naïve individuals of Gondar teaching hospital (G) and Metema hospital (M) in northwest Ethiopia. Sequences with primary drug resistance mutations are indicated (black circles). For comparison 9 reference strains (empty circles) from the Los Alamos HIV-1 database representing subtype C with GenBank accession numbers U46016 (Ethiopia C), AF067155 (India C), AF110980 (Botswana C), AF361874 (Tanzania C), AY772699 (S Africa C), U52953 (Brazil C), AF286233 (Israel C), AF286230 (China C), AF286225 (Zambia C) are included. Sequences of this study were submitted to GenBank with the accession numbers KJ807649–KJ807731.
