The SW participant harbored a CRF01_AE strain, which bootstrap values indicate varied little from genotypes previously described in Southeast Asia. 13 Of interest, the SW genotype identified did not match that in the IDU networks in Kabul and Hirat; this may indicate limited mixing between IDU and SW networks, although our small sample precludes more definitive inferences.
